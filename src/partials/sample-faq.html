What is the New Tool?

New Tool integrates all secondary functions from pricing to funding and helps you easily deliver content to us under a variety of executions.

How do I get started with the New Tool?

To start using New Tool you'll need to provide us with information regarding authorized New Tool users and their roles. You submit this information by completing and submitting the required forms.

What New Tool training or resources are available?

We offer multiple training resources and opportunities. Please see the Training Site for more information, including self-study tutorials available 24/7 and training for new users.

What information do I need to use your new tool?

The Instructions contains our requirements for the purchase, sale and servicing of content. For more information on how to access the Instructions, visit our web page. We periodically publish updates to these requirements through Letters. To receive notice of these updates and other important information, visit the Subscription Page.

For more information on specific data fields that must be delivered in the New Tool, visit our Dataset page.

Please click here for New Tool hours of operation, and be sure to sign up for our Alerts to stay updated on any developments.
