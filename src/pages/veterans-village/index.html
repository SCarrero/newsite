The challenge is massive, but the mission is simple.

Veterans Village was founded to provide reliable housing for those who have served our country. With help from Freddie Mac and Berkadia, they have helped tens of thousands of veterans and their families find a place to call home.

A New Start

Home is more than a roof over your head—it’s a place where you feel safe, supported, and cared for. At Veterans Village, residents are supported holistically, with access to an on-site food pantry, therapy, job counseling, a medical clinic and more.
