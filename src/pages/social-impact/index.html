What is it?

Social impact financing is an investment strategy focused on generating a valuable social impact while realizing a financial return – the double bottom line. 

More and more, multifamily investors are realizing the benefits of social impact investing. Freddie Mac is well-positioned to lead this important movement, leveraging private capital for public benefit.

We're doing our part by providing value-enhanced solutions to the affordability crisis. We provide flexible financing structures with attractive spreads to encourage impact investors to support affordable and workforce_housing_mezz housing.

Impact Loans

Aimed at solving the workforce housing crisis, Borrowers preserve rents through voluntary restrictions to support low-income and working families over the life of the loan.

More

Mezzanine Loans

We offer mezzanine loans for both workforce housing and affordable housing. These products help preserve affordable housing by offering favorable financing terms to the Borrowers and maintaining affordable rents for the life of the loan.

Conventional Targeted Affordable Housing

NOAH Preservation

Supports long-term preservation of affordable housing. Features underwriting flexibilities, fee reductions and rehabilitation allowance.
